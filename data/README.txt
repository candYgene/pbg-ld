Tomato/potato genome annotations and QTLs are freely available at Zenodo:

[1] Kuzniar, Arnold. (2019). Genome annotations of Solanaceae species (Version 1.0) [Data set]. Zenodo. doi:10.5281/zenodo.3383758

[2] Kuzniar, Arnold, & Singh, Gurnoor. (2019). Quantitative Trait Loci of Solanaceae species (Version 1.1) [Data set]. Zenodo. doi:10.5281/zenodo.3383307

The domain-specific vocabularies/ontologies, Ensembl Plants and UniProt Proteomes can be downloaded using the 'get_rdf.sh' script in the 'src' directory.
